Privacy Policy - Soak Games

Privacy Policy

Last updated November, 14th 2023

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected and processed by Soak Games when you use our applications.

Remit of the personal data protection policy

This personal data protection policy is applicable to all of our applications.

What data is collected?

  • Identity-related data (e.g. name, email, profile info when using Facebook login)
  • IDFA (iOS) and GAID (Android)
  • Data about your activity on our apps and how you interact with ads
  • Geographical position (IP address)

We also use cookies and external SDKs for analytics and ads, such as:

  • Games Analytics (analytical SDK)
  • Facebook (analytical SDK)
  • Admob (SDK advertising)

Purposes of processing by Soak Games

  • Provide our applications
  • Guarantee use and improvement of our applications
  • Develop new applications
  • Understand your interests to propose targeted and relevant advertisements
  • Statistics on your use of the games
  • Communication when you contact us

Term of data storage

We store data for a maximum of 13 months after collection, after which it may be anonymized or deleted.

Categories of recipients

Data is shared only with internal teams and technical service providers under strict confidentiality. It may be disclosed:

  • If required by law
  • For national security or legal proceedings
  • In the event of transfer, merger, or sale

Data transfer

Data may be transferred outside the EU through certified entities under Privacy Shield.

Personal data concerning children

Soak Games does not knowingly collect data from children under 15 years old.

Your rights concerning data collected

  • Right to confirmation of processing
  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to object or limit processing
  • Right to data portability

Personal data security

We implement measures to prevent unauthorized access or alteration. If a breach occurs, we will notify you if required.

Request user data deletion

To delete user data collected by Soak Games, please reach out to info@soakgames.com.

For third-party SDKs, contact their support directly:

  • GameAnalytics: privacy@gameanalytics.com
  • Facebook Analytics: see their privacy policy
  • Adjust: privacy@adjust.com
  • Voodoo: dpo@voodoo.io
